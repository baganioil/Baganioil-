Bagani is proud to announce the launch of its newest product, the Amihan Tempest 4T Premium Motorcycle Oil. Developed after two years of intensive research and testing, the Amihan Tempest represents a breakthrough in motorcycle lubricant technology designed specifically for the demanding conditions of Philippine roads.

The Amihan Tempest 4T features an advanced synthetic blend formulation that provides superior engine protection even in extreme heat and heavy traffic conditions common in Metro Manila and other urban centers across the Philippines. Its unique additive package reduces friction by up to 15% compared to conventional motorcycle oils, resulting in smoother engine performance and improved fuel efficiency.

"The Amihan Tempest is our most advanced formulation yet. We designed it specifically for the Filipino rider who needs reliable engine protection in the toughest conditions. This is truly Gawang Pilipino, Para sa Pilipino." - Marco Reyes, CEO of Bagani

Key features of the Amihan Tempest 4T include enhanced thermal stability that maintains viscosity even at high operating temperatures, advanced anti-wear additives that extend engine life, and improved detergent properties that keep engines clean and running at peak performance.

Product highlights and availability

The Amihan Tempest 4T is available in multiple viscosity grades to suit different motorcycle types and riding conditions. Whether you ride a scooter for daily commuting or a big bike for weekend adventures, there is an Amihan Tempest formulation perfect for your needs.

  • Available in 10W-40 and 15W-50 viscosity grades
  • Meets API SN and JASO MA2 specifications
  • Enhanced thermal stability for Philippine climate
  • Advanced anti-wear protection for extended engine life
  • Compatible with both wet clutch and dry clutch systems

The Amihan Tempest 4T is now available at authorized Bagani dealers and motorcycle shops nationwide. With a suggested retail price that is competitive with imported brands, Bagani continues its mission of providing world-class motorcycle oils at prices accessible to every Filipino rider. Visit your nearest Bagani dealer or contact us to learn more about this exciting new product.
