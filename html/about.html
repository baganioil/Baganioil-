our history

The Bagani journey

From humble beginnings in the Philippines, Bagani has grown into a trusted name in motorcycle lubricants, driven by a passion for quality and a commitment to serving Filipino riders nationwide.

The idea begins

A group of Filipino engineers and entrepreneurs identified the need for a high-quality, locally-made motorcycle oil brand. Inspired by the Filipino warrior spirit, the name "Bagani" was chosen to represent strength and resilience.

  • Market Research & Planning
  • Formulation Development
  • Filipino Manufacturing Vision
  • Brand Identity Creation

Brand launch

Bagani officially launched its first line of motorcycle oils, starting with the Amihan series. The brand quickly gained traction among local motorcycle communities in Metro Manila and surrounding provinces.

  • First Product Line Released
  • Local Distribution Network
  • Community Partnerships
  • Quality Certification

Nationwide reach

Bagani expanded its distribution network across the Philippines, partnering with motorcycle shops and retailers in Visayas and Mindanao. The brand became a household name among Filipino riders.

  • Visayas & Mindanao Expansion
  • Retail Partner Network Growth
  • Rider Community Events
  • Product Line Diversification

Product expansion

Bagani introduced new product lines including transmission fluids, coolants, and specialty lubricants. The company invested in advanced manufacturing facilities to meet growing demand.

  • New Product Categories
  • Manufacturing Facility Upgrade
  • Quality Lab Expansion
  • Industry Certifications

Industry leader

Today, Bagani stands as one of the leading Filipino motorcycle oil brands, trusted by thousands of riders nationwide. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and Filipino pride continues to drive us forward.

  • Market Leadership Position
  • Southeast Asia Expansion Plans
  • Sustainable Manufacturing
  • Community Impact Programs
